Hobbies

Game Development

On the side I enjoy working with Godot, Unity, and a little bit with GameMaker Studio 2. While working in a 3D environment is tricky, I'm getting the hang of 2D pretty well and have made a couple mini projects which include a weather system complete with seasons and climate based weather and a time cycle to go along with it. As of now, I'm working on a top-down terrain generator with biomes, the only tricky part is figuring out bitmasking and using that to assign the correct tile piece (autotiling).

Pixel Art

I also enjoy working on pixel art. While art has never been a strong suite of mine, it goes hand-in-hand with game development! I can draw some pretty decent landscape/nature pieces, however, characters are still difficult for me to wrap my head (and hand) around. Especially when it comes to animating those images!

Particle Effects

Particle effects is relatively new in terms of a hobby for me. I messed around with them when I got started in game development. While it is time consuming, seeing the results after hours of micro-managing values is well worth it! One of my favorite "types" of particle effects is using custom sprites (2D images) and using those as particles. For example, leaves falling from a tree.

Carpentry

I've been doing carpentry for a couple years as well. Using AutoCAD or good ole fashioned pen and paper, my Dad and I have made book shelf's, desks, T.V. stands, and a small dresser. I could never do it as a career, but it is ridiculously fun making something I need and will use quite often.

Outdoors

Hiking, fishing, hunting, and stargazing are my top favorite things to do outdoors, especially after I moved up to Idaho. Something I would like to do sooner rather than later is deep sea fishing. Reeling in a large mouth bass or catfish has its moments, but catching a shark, tuna, or really anything in the ocean would be tremendously fun! I would put skiing on the list, but, I have yet to ski anything but a bunny hill!

Gaming

I hop online whenever I have the time away from school, work, and other commitments. Normally, I play tactical, first person shooters, or real time strategy games with my brother. Some of our recent ones include Rainbow Six Siege, Valorant, CS:GO, Factorio, and Sons of the Forest. After all, if one of my hobbies is making games, you bet your biscuits playing them is not far behind!
